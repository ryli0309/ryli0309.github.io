HOW TO SUBMIT TO US

Unfortunately, the submission period for Volume XX has been closed.

Three Rivers Review accepts unpublished work by undergraduates at institutions of post-secondary education in the Greater Pittsburgh area. We do not accept simultaneous submissions. All submissions are reviewed in anonymity.

Submissions are opened and reviewed between September 15 and January 15. Responses to submitted work can be expected by March 1, following the reading period.

Submit up to 15 pages (Times New Roman 12-point font, double spaced) of fiction or 5 poems as a Microsoft Word (.doc) file to:

ThreeRiversReview@gmail.com

In the subject heading of the email, please type "TRR Poetry Submission" or "TRR Fiction Submission," depending on which you are submitting. In the body of the email, please include your name, contact information, and a short, ready-to-print bio about yourself. As a last reminder: please do NOT put your name in the attached submission document.

Prizes: $250 to the winner and $100 to the runner up of each competition.

If you have any other questions, comments, or concerns, please don't hesitate to contact us at our email: ThreeRiversReview@gmail.com, or visit us at our Facebook page!
