GOOD MORNING

First opened in 1950 and a Pittsburgh classic ever since, Ritter's Diner provides a quintessential diner experience with its casual, no-frills service, all-day breakfast menu, and nostalgic atmosphere. It's the place to go when you want to be alone but not lonely, or when you want to have brunch with friends on a Sunday morning. It's the place to fall in love over bottomless coffee and the place to go for drunk food at 3 AM, bumping elbows and licking ketchup off your fingers.
